# Equipment Progression Guide

**Character**: Generic Cleric (Minotaur Warpriest with Dragonblood Heritage)
**Build Focus**: Unarmed combat (Handwraps of Mighty Blows), Healing, Survivability
**Deity**: Irori (Favored Weapon: Fist/Unarmed Strikes)

This guide shows the recommended equipment progression from level 1 to 20, based on the official Pathfinder Builder export and PF2e item level requirements.

---

## Level 1-2: Starting Equipment

### Weapons
- **Horns** (Natural weapon from Minotaur ancestry)
  - 1d8 piercing unarmed attack
  - Free action

### Armor
- **Breastplate** or **Chain Mail** (Medium armor - Warpriest proficiency)
  - AC Bonus: +4
  - Dex Cap: +1 (Breastplate) or +1 (Chain Mail)
  - Check Penalty: -2
  - Speed Penalty: -5 ft
  - Source: Player Core pg. 273

### Shield
- **Basic Wooden Shield** or **Steel Shield**
  - AC Bonus: +2 (when raised)
  - Hardness: 3 (wood) or 5 (steel)
  - HP: 12 (wood) or 20 (steel)
  - BT: 6 (wood) or 10 (steel)

### Wealth
- Spend starting gold (15 gp) on:
  - Healing tools (Healer's Tools: 5 gp)
  - Adventurer's Pack (7 sp)
  - Rope, rations, torches, etc.

## Property Runes (Optional)

### Weapon Property Runes (for Handwraps)
- **Flaming** (level 8, 500 gp): +1d6 fire damage
- **Holy** (level 11, 1,400 gp): +1d6 vitality damage vs unholy creatures
- **Striking Runes**: Already maxed out with Major Striking

### Armor Property Runes (for Lattice Armor)
- **Energy-Resistant** (level 8, 460 gp): Resistance 5 to chosen energy type
- **Shadow** (level 9, 650 gp): +1 item bonus to Stealth checks

### Shield Property Runes (for Salvo Shield)
- **Arrow-Catching** (level 7, 360 gp): +2 to AC against ranged attacks when raised
- Already has Supreme Reinforcing (primary defensive rune)

## Notes on Build Choices

### Why Handwraps Over Traditional Weapons?
1. **Deity Alignment**: Irori's favored weapon is Fist (unarmed strikes)
2. **Natural Weapons**: Minotaur Horns (1d8 piercing) are excellent
3. **Versatility**: Can switch between Horns, Fists, Kicks as needed
4. **Replenishment of War Feat**: Gain temp HP when hitting with deity's weapon

### Why Lattice Armor?
1. **Better Dex Cap**: +2 vs +1 for most medium armor
2. **Composite Group**: Unique benefits
3. **Cost-Effective**: Only 45 gp at level 5
4. **Aesthetic**: Intricate metal plates fit character theme

### Why Salvo Shield?
1. **Superior Stats**: Better than basic shields
2. **Reinforcing Compatibility**: Can add Supreme Reinforcing
3. **Combat Utility**: Works with ranged weapons if needed
4. **Durability**: High HP and Hardness when fully upgraded

## Alternative Equipment Paths

### For More Offense (Property Runes)
- **Holy Weapon Rune** (level 11): +1d6 vitality vs unholy
- **Flaming Weapon Rune** (level 8): +1d6 fire damage
- **Grievous Weapon Rune** (level 9): Extra persistent bleed damage on crit

### For More Defense
- **Fortification Armor Rune** (level 12): Reduce crit damage
- **Energy-Resistant Armor** (level 8): Resist 5 to chosen energy
- **Greater Energy-Resistant** (level 12): Resist 10 to chosen energy

### For Utility
- **Boots of Speed** (level 13): +5 ft status bonus to Speed
- **Cloak of the Bat** (level 10): Fly as bat once per day
- **Bracers of Armor** (level varies): Armor bonus for unarmored builds
